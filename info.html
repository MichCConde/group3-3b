Meet the Team!

EcoHealth is a project created by Group 3 BSN 3B as part of the Nursing Informatics course. Our team—Bridgette Jance Agura, Emman Benedict Arias, Hyacinth Batutay Kienz, Noelle Kafka Benitez, Kassandra Chiong, Jean Aruel Pangilinan, Angel Romulito Pielago, and Marie Joy Tumulak—developed this website to explore the innovative field of ecotechnology and its impact on health. EcoHealth serves as a comprehensive resource to educate and inspire others about sustainable technologies that improve healthcare and protect our environment.
