About Us

Welcome to our page! We are Group 3 from BSN 3B, a passionate team of students dedicated to exploring and promoting ecotechnology in health. Our mission is to uncover innovative technologies that enhance healthcare sustainability and improve patient outcomes. Through our research and initiatives, we aim to highlight the critical role of eco-friendly technologies in creating healthier, more sustainable healthcare environments. Join us as we delve into the fascinating intersection of ecology and technology, and discover how it can revolutionize health and well-being for communities around the world.
